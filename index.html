Discovering the Bay Area

The Bay Area is famously known for the Golden Bay Bridge, progressive politics, the Golden State Warriors and the SF Giants, it's hippie (and hyphy) culture, and for being the mecca of the tech industry. Another great reason to visit the Bay Area is for it's prestigious Arts & Wine Festivals. These events are free and take place during the summer and fall throughout Silicon Valley.
