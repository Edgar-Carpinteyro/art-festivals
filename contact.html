Interested in these festivals?

Click on the links below to learn more about these amazing festivals.

Los Gatos Art & Wine Festival

It takes place on the Civic Center lawn in beautiful downtown Los Gatos at 110 E. Main St. Featuring over 200 artists, live music all day/all weekend, fine wine by local vintners and craft beer. Not to mention food trucks and plenty of opportunities to have a great time. This is an event not to be missed!

Mountain View Art & Wine Festival

It's Silicon Valley’s World-Class Art, Music & Food Festival, voted Best Festival by Metro readership for more than a decade. The festival features 300 of America’s finest artists and craftmakers showing exceptional handcrafted wares and Stellar live music on stage and street throughout downtown.

Palo Alto Art & Wine Festival

This high-quality festival, hosted by the Palo Alto Chamber of Commerce, attracts over 300 fine art & contemporary craft displays, over 60 Italian Street Painters, Kids' Art Studio, specialty areas, stage & street entertainment, gourmet food, fine wines & micro-brews.

Los Altos Art & Wine Festival

Downtown Los Altos will host one of the largest two-day, events in the Bay Area. The highly-rated Los Altos Arts & Wine Festival celebrates its 43rd year and showcases the distinctive handcrafted works of artists and craftspeople from more than a dozen states.

Fremont Art & Wine Festival

The Fremont Festival of The Arts is the largest free street festival west of the Mississippi. It attracts over 300,000 people annually and has activities and entertainment for the entire family.
